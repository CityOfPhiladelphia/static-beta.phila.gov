Scheduling Request Form

  • Step 1: Introduction

  • Please complete as much of this form as possible. Forms with more detailed responses will be easier to assess, and will allow the scheduling staffto respond more promptly.

    • After you submit your event request, you will receive an automatic e-mail from the scheduling staff confirming that we have received the request. Please note that completing this form does not guarantee a representative'sparticipation or attendance at your event.
    • The scheduling staffwill respond to all requests within 7-14 days. If the scheduling office e-mails you requesting more details for your event, please allow another 7 days for a response to your request.
    • Please submit requests at least 2-3 weeks prior to the events occurrence.
    • Due to executive order 3-11 (Prohibition of Acceptance of Gifts by City Officers and Employees), representatives are unable to accept any gifts at events. Please do not present the representativewith any items of value.
